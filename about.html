About Pet Cuddle Clinic

A place where pets feel safe, loved, and understood — created with the dream of delivering gentle, stress-free, compassionate care.

Our Story

Pet Cuddle Clinic was created with one simple dream — to bring gentle, stress-free, and compassionate care to every pet who walks through our doors.

We believe animals are not just pets… they’re family. They deserve the same comfort, patience, and love as any human. Every experience here is designed to feel safe, warm, and caring — just like a cuddle.

Pet Cuddle Clinic began as a small idea in a small room — to build a space where animals feel comforted, not scared. Over the years, we grew into a full-service clinic with trained doctors, modern facilities, and a deep passion for animal wellbeing.

Our team consists of people who don’t just treat pets — they understand them, comfort them, and care for them like family.

🏥 Our Facilities

🐾 Facilities for Pets

  • Calm Examination Rooms — pastel walls, soft lighting, and cozy bedding to reduce stress for pets.
  • Grooming & Spa Zone — gentle baths, coat treatments, nail care, and hydrobath for complete comfort.
  • Advanced Diagnostics Lab — quick blood tests, urine analysis, and same-day reports.
  • Digital Imaging Suite — high-quality X-rays and ultrasound for accurate scanning.
  • Comfort Recovery Rooms — temperature-controlled resting areas with regular monitoring.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Facilities for Pet Owners

  • Private Consultation Rooms — speak with the vet comfortably and understand treatments clearly.
  • Online Appointment System — easy booking, reminders, and follow-up messages.
  • Relaxing Waiting Lounge — soft seating with refreshments while your pet is being treated.
  • Pet Care Education Corner — nutrition, behavior, first-aid guidance, and training tips.
  • Vet-Approved Mini Store — safe pet foods, treats, toys, and supplements recommended by our team.
